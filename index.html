Art of a Typeface

Typography study and design folio for SACE Standards. 2016

A Typeface is the alphabet in clothing. Letters are always shown, but never naked. They are always in a typeface, and how a typeface is designed can effect the text that it embodies. This Design folio showcases how Typefaces can be created and manipulated to create artwork and emotion, while still being as plain as text. This received an A+ Grade under SACE Standards. Distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution Liscence.

Func

A Graphic Design language based around a common shape. 2015

Func is a Design language based around conforming to a single shape. Humans like familiarity, and by using the same shape at the center of all your work, the design will be able to be read easier, look more appealing and get the message across. An example of Func in practice is Mello, my Icon pack.
